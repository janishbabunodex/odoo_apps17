🗓️ HR Leave Resumption Date

Track and manage employee rejoin & resumption dates seamlessly within Odoo's Time Off module.

Odoo 17 HR / Time Off Free & Open Source LGPL-3

Overview

HR Leave Resumption Date extends Odoo's native Time Off (hr_holidays) module to give HR managers a clear, validated record of when employees are expected — and actually do — return to work after approved leave. It also tracks flight-ticket eligibility automatically.

Features

📅 Resumption Date Field

Add an expected return-to-work date directly on the leave request form, with full chatter tracking.

✅ Date Validation

Prevents saving a resumption date that falls before the leave end date, with a clear error message.

➕ Create Resumption Leave

One-click button (visible after leave approval) creates a linked Unpaid leave record for the resumption period.

🔗 Smart Button

"View Resumption Leave" smart button appears once a resumption record is linked, for quick navigation.

👤 Rejoin Date on Employee

Store the employee's actual rejoin date on their profile, with tracking history in the chatter.

✈️ Flight Ticket Eligibility

Automatically computes the flight ticket eligible date as two years after the rejoin date.

How It Works

  1. HR manager opens an approved leave request.
  2. Sets a Resumption Date on the leave form.
  3. A confirmation warning is shown; the date is saved with tracking.
  4. After validation, the Create Resumption Leave button appears in the header.
  5. Clicking it creates a linked Unpaid leave record from the day after leave ends to the resumption date.
  6. The View Resumption Leave smart button lets you navigate directly to the linked record.
  7. On the Employee profile, set the Rejoin Date; the Flight Ticket Eligible Date is computed automatically.
Note: The "Create Resumption Leave" button is only visible when the leave is in Validated state and a Resumption Date has been set.

Installation

  1. Download and unzip into your Odoo addons directory.
  2. Restart the Odoo service.
  3. Activate Developer Mode.
  4. Go to Apps → Update App List.
  5. Search for HR Leave Resumption Date and click Install.
